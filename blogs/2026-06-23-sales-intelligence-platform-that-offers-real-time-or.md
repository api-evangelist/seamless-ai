Seamless AI launched Org Charts, a feature that lets sales teams identify key decision-makers and buying committees within target companies by searching organizational structures filtered by seniority or department. The tool streamlines target account selling, account-based marketing, and customer account management by letting users build prospect lists and automate research with contact information.
